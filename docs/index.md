# Golden Palace

> _"I have hidden your word in my heart that I might not sin against you."_
> — Psalm 119:11

A scripture memorization system built on Hebrew pictographs, gematria, PAO library, and mind palace — so head knowledge becomes **heart knowledge**, the Bible comes alive, and people are transformed by Jesus.

## What Is This?

The **Golden Palace** is a knowledge repository for memorizing and internalizing God's Word. The name is intentional: gold was the material of the Holy of Holies, the inner sanctuary where God's presence dwelt. This is that inner sanctuary in digital form — for the joy of reading and studying His Word, and to equip people for the spiritual battles ahead.

**If you find memorizing hard** — especially with ADHD — you were not made incorrectly. You are not "not His sheep." This system exists to help you hide the Word in your heart. It is designed with vivid images, small steps, and clear pegs so that what works for neurodivergent learners works even better for everyone.

It combines:

- **Ancient Hebrew memory methods** — the way Jewish boys memorized the entire Torah by age 12
- **Modern memory athlete techniques** — PAO system, mind palace, spaced repetition
- **Hebrew pictographic theology** — every letter is a picture, every picture is a sermon
- **Gematria** — the numerical architecture God embedded in the Hebrew text

## Quick Start

Start with the [Hebrew Alphabet](/alphabet/) to learn the foundational letters that make this system work. For the full sequence, see [Learning path](/techniques/#learning-path-best-order) on the Techniques page.

## How We Teach

We write like a **good teacher**: clear, kind, structured. We use **analogies** everywhere (e.g. mind palace = same cupboard for groceries; PAO = Rolodex of tiny movies). The material is designed **neurodivergent-first** — explicit steps, multi-sensory hooks, short wins — so it tends to work even better for neurotypical learners. The goal is **equipping**, not performance.

## The Aleph-Tav Signature

Genesis 1:1 contains a word that no English Bible translates:

**אֵת** (Aleph-Tav), the first and last letters of the Hebrew alphabet, appearing silently over 7,000 times in the Hebrew Bible.

> _"I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last, the Beginning and the End."_
> — Revelation 22:13

In Hebrew: _"I am the Aleph and the Tav."_

From the first sentence of Genesis, before the law, before the prophets, before Bethlehem, He signed His name into the text.

> _"It is the glory of God to conceal a matter; to search out a matter is the glory of kings."_
> — Proverbs 25:2
