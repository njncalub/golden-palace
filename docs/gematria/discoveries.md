# Gematria Discoveries

A collection of gematria connections and numerical patterns discovered while studying Scripture. These are devotional insights, not doctrinal foundations.

## Word Connections

### Love (אהב) and One (אחד)

- **Ahavah** (אהבה) — Love: 1 + 5 + 2 + 5 = **13**
- **Echad** (אחד) — One: 1 + 8 + 4 = **13**

Love and oneness share the same value. The Shema says: _"The LORD our God, the LORD is one"_ (Deuteronomy 6:4) and we are called to love God with everything (Deuteronomy 6:5). Love and oneness are numerically linked.

### Light (אור) and Secret (רז)

- **Or** (אור) — Light: 1 + 6 + 200 = **207**
- **Raz** (רז) — Secret: 200 + 7 = **207**

Light reveals secrets. God is light and in Him is no darkness at all (1 John 1:5).

## Number Patterns

### The Number 37 — The Heart of Wisdom

- **Yechida** (יחידה) — Unity, oneness: 10 + 8 + 10 + 4 + 5 = **37**
- Appears in key Hebrew words related to wisdom and understanding

### The Number 26 — The Name

- **YHWH** (יהוה) — The sacred name: 10 + 5 + 6 + 5 = **26**
- God's covenant name carries this value throughout Scripture

## Verse Numerical Patterns

### Genesis 1:1 — The Foundation

_In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth._ (Bereshit bara Elohim et ha-shamayim v'et ha-aretz)

The Hebrew letters of this verse reveal mathematical patterns that have fascinated scholars for centuries. The verse contains:

- **7 words** — the number of spiritual perfection
- **28 letters** (7 × 4) — creation completeness
- The first and last letters spell **et** (את) — the sign of the direct object, Jesus the Aleph and Tav

### Isaiah 53 — The Suffering Servant

Chapter 53 has 12 verses. The chapter speaks of the Messiah who would suffer for our sins. Twelve is the number of governmental perfection — Jesus is the King who suffered to establish His kingdom.

## Caution

Remember: gematria illuminates, it doesn't legislate. These patterns are beautiful and can deepen worship, but they should never be used to build doctrine that isn't clearly taught elsewhere in Scripture.

> _"All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness."_
> — 2 Timothy 3:16

The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing: Jesus.
