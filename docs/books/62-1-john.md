# 1 John

**TLDR:** We have heard, seen, touched the Word of life. God is light; God is love. Walk in the light; love one another; believe in the name of the Son. "Perfect love drives out fear." Assurance and obedience together.

## Overarching Storyline

Word of life; God is light (ch. 1–2). Do not love the world; antichrists (ch. 2). Children of God; love one another (ch. 3). Test the spirits; God is love (ch. 4). Faith and assurance (ch. 5).

**Bible Project:** [1 John overview](https://bibleproject.com/explore/video/1-john/).

## Pegs for Memorizing This Book

- **Person:** John; antichrists; children of God.
- **Image:** Light, love, abiding.
- **Number:** 5 (chapters).
- **Phrase:** "God is love" (4:8, 16); "Perfect love drives out fear" (4:18); "If we confess our sins" (1:9).

## Highlights

- 1 John 1:9 — If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just and will forgive us our sins.
- 1 John 3:1 — See what great love the Father has lavished on us, that we should be called children of God.
- 1 John 4:8 — Whoever does not love does not know God, because God is love.
- 1 John 5:14 — If we ask anything according to his will, he hears us.

## Before and After

- **Before:** 2 Peter; 1 John is pastoral and doctrinal — assurance and love.
- **After:** 2 John; 1 John is the longest of the three; 2 and 3 John are brief.

## Place in the Overarching Biblical Story

**Assurance and love.** We know we have eternal life (5:13); we love because He first loved us. The letter counters docetism and encourages fellowship with God and one another.

## Interesting Facts

- **No author named** — Tradition attributes to the apostle John; style is close to the Gospel of John.
- **God is love** — 1 John 4:8, 16; love is not just an attribute but God's nature.
