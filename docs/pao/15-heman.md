# 15 — Person: Heman (singer)

**Number:** 15

**Person:** Heman (the Ezrahite, Levite singer)

**Action:** Leading the songs of ascent with cymbals (temple worship)

**Object:** Cymbals / temple steps

**Scripture connection:** 1 Chronicles 15:17 — Heman among the Levites with musical instruments; Psalm 88 title — "A song. A psalm of the sons of Korah. For the director. According to mahalath leannoth. A maskil of Heman the Ezrahite." Fifteen Songs of Ascents (Psalms 120–134).

**Memory hook:** 15 = the fifteen Songs of Ascents (Psalms 120–134). Heman the singer — cymbals, steps, the Levite who leads the people upward in worship. One singer, one number for the steps to the house of the LORD.

**Why this number?** 15 = the fifteen Songs of Ascents (Psalms 120–134); Heman the Ezrahite is the Levite singer (1 Chronicles 15:17; Psalm 88 title). The number 15 ties temple song and the steps of ascent to the house of the LORD.
