# 14 — Person: The Israelite (Passover)

**Number:** 14

**Person:** The Israelite (head of household at the first Passover)

**Action:** Applying blood to the doorposts and lintel

**Object:** Passover lamb / blood in a basin, hyssop

**Scripture connection:** Exodus 12:6–7 — On the fourteenth day of the first month at twilight they kill the lamb and put the blood on the two doorposts and the lintel of the house; Exodus 12:13 — the blood is a sign; the LORD will pass over.

**Memory hook:** 14th of Nisan — the day the lamb is killed and the blood applied. One night, one act of obedience — the door marked so that death passes over. Fourteen locks the date in place.

**Why this number?** 14th of Nisan — Exodus 12:6. The Passover lamb is killed and the blood applied to the doorposts on the fourteenth; the Israelite who obeys is spared when the LORD passes over.
