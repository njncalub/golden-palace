# 36 — Person: Zephaniah

**Number:** 36

**Person:** Zephaniah

**Action:** Prophesying over Jerusalem — "He will rejoice over you with singing"

**Object:** Jerusalem / scroll

**Scripture connection:** Zephaniah 3:17 — "The LORD your God is in your midst, a mighty one who will save; he will rejoice over you with gladness; he will quiet you by his love; he will exult over you with loud singing."

**Memory hook:** Zeph 3:17 — "He will rejoice over you." The number 36 ties the prophet to Jerusalem and the scroll — one concrete scene: the LORD singing over His people.

**Why this number?** Zephaniah 3:17 — "He will rejoice over you with singing"; the number 36 with concrete peg: city and scroll.
