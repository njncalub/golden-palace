# 94 — Person: Cornelius

**Number:** 94

**Person:** Cornelius

**Action:** Sending for Peter (the centurion who feared God; his messengers and prayer)

**Object:** Messengers / prayer

**Scripture connection:** Acts 10:1–2 — At Caesarea there was a man named Cornelius, a centurion of what was known as the Italian Cohort, a devout man who feared God with all his household. Acts 10:7–8 — When the angel who spoke to him had departed, he called two of his servants and a devout soldier … and sent them to Joppa.

**Memory hook:** 94 = Tet-Dalet = basket + door; Acts 10. Cornelius and the messengers — the number 94 is the Gentile whose house received the Spirit and opened the door to the nations.

**Why this number?** 94 = Tet-Dalet = basket + door; Acts 10. Cornelius sent for Peter; the number 94 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the door to the Gentiles.
