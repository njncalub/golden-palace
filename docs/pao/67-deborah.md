# 67 — Person: Deborah

**Number:** 67

**Person:** Deborah

**Action:** Judging Israel under the palm of Deborah (between Ramah and Bethel)

**Object:** Palm of Deborah

**Scripture connection:** Judges 4:4 — Now Deborah, a prophetess, the wife of Lappidoth, was judging Israel at that time. She used to sit under the palm of Deborah between Ramah and Bethel in the hill country of Ephraim.

**Memory hook:** 67 = Vav-Zayin = nail/weapon; judge (Judg 4). Deborah is the prophetess and judge under the palm; the number 67 ties her to the Hebrew letters and the palm.

**Why this number?** 67 = Vav-Zayin = nail/weapon; judge (Judg 4). Deborah judged under the palm; the number 67 ties her to the letters and the scene.
