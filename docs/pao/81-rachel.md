# 81 — Person: Rachel

**Number:** 81

**Person:** Rachel

**Action:** Weeping for her children (the voice heard in Ramah — prophecy in Jeremiah and Matthew)

**Object:** Tomb / Ramah

**Scripture connection:** Jeremiah 31:15 — Thus says the LORD: "A voice is heard in Ramah, lamentation and bitter weeping. Rachel is weeping for her children; she refuses to be comforted for her children, because they are no more." Matthew 2:18 — quoted at the slaughter of the innocents.

**Memory hook:** 81 = Chet-Aleph = fence + first; Jer 31:15. Rachel weeping — the number 81 is the mother and the voice in Ramah.

**Why this number?** 81 = Chet-Aleph = fence + first; Jer 31:15. Rachel weeping for her children; the number 81 ties her to the Hebrew letters and Ramah.
