# 07 — Person: Joshua

**Number:** 07

**Person:** Joshua

**Action:** Marching around Jericho (seventh day, seven circuits, trumpets)

**Object:** Trumpet (shofar) / ark of the covenant

**Scripture connection:** Joshua 6:4, 15–16 — March around the city seven days; on the seventh day march seven times, then the wall will fall.

**Memory hook:** Zayin = weapon, crown. The weapon here is the shout and the trumpet; the seventh day is the day of victory. Seven = completeness — Jericho falls when the pattern is complete.

**Why this number?** 7 maps to Zayin (ז) — weapon, crown. Joshua leads Israel to march around Jericho for seven days; on the seventh day they march seven times and the walls fall. Seven is the number of completion and divine victory (weapon of the LORD).
