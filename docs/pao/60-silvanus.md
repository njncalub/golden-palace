# 60 — Person: Silvanus

**Number:** 60

**Person:** Silvanus

**Action:** Carrying Peter's letter to the exiles (scribe and faithful brother)

**Object:** Letter (1 Peter)

**Scripture connection:** 1 Peter 5:12 — By Silvanus, a faithful brother as I regard him, I have written briefly to you, exhorting and declaring that this is the true grace of God. Stand firm in it.

**Memory hook:** 60 = 1 Peter; Silvanus the scribe/carrier (1 Pet 5:12). The number 60 is the one named person who carried the first letter of Peter to the exiles.

**Why this number?** 60 = 1 Peter; Silvanus the scribe/carrier (1 Pet 5:12). Silvanus carried Peter's letter; the number 60 ties him to 1 Peter.
