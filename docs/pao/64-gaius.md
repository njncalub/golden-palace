# 64 — Person: Gaius

**Number:** 64

**Person:** Gaius

**Action:** Showing hospitality to the brothers and strangers (walking in the truth)

**Object:** Hospitality

**Scripture connection:** 3 John 1 — The elder to the beloved Gaius, whom I love in truth. 3 John 5 — Beloved, it is a faithful thing you do in all your efforts for these brothers, strangers as they are.

**Memory hook:** 64 = 3 John; Gaius (64). Gaius is the one to whom the elder wrote — the beloved who showed hospitality. The number 64 is the host and the letter.

**Why this number?** 64 = 3 John; Gaius (64). Gaius showed hospitality; the number 64 ties him to the third letter of John.
