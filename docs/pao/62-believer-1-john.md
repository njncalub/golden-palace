# 62 — Person: A believer (1 John)

**Number:** 62

**Person:** A believer (the "we" who confess and receive forgiveness in 1 John)

**Action:** Confessing sins; receiving forgiveness and cleansing (1 John 1:9)

**Object:** Forgiveness / light

**Scripture connection:** 1 John 1:9 — If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness. 1 John 1:7 — But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another.

**Memory hook:** 62 = 1 John; one believer, concrete action — confessing and receiving forgiveness. The number 62 avoids duplicating John (43); one person in the "we" who walks in the light.

**Why this number?** 62 = 1 John; one believer, concrete action. The number 62 ties the act of confessing and receiving forgiveness to the first letter of John.
