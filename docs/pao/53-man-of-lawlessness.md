# 53 — Person: The man of lawlessness

**Number:** 53

**Person:** The man of lawlessness (the one who exalts himself in the temple)

**Action:** Sitting in the temple of God, exalting himself (as described in 2 Thessalonians)

**Object:** Temple

**Scripture connection:** 2 Thessalonians 2:4 — He opposes and exalts himself against every so-called god or object of worship, so that he takes his seat in the temple of God, proclaiming himself to be God.

**Memory hook:** 53 = 2 Thessalonians; 2 Thess 2:4 — the concrete scene of the man of lawlessness in the temple. The number 53 is the vivid image of the one who sets himself in God's place.

**Why this number?** 53 = 2 Thessalonians; 2 Thess 2:4 — concrete scene. The number 53 ties the man of lawlessness to the second letter and the temple.
