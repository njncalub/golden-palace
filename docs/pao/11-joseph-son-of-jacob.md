# 11 — Person: Joseph (son of Jacob)

**Number:** 11

**Person:** Joseph (son of Jacob)

**Action:** Telling his dream of eleven stars bowing to him

**Object:** Eleven stars (sun, moon, eleven stars)

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 37:9 — "He had another dream, and he told it to his brothers. 'Listen,' he said, 'I had another dream: the sun, the moon, and eleven stars were bowing down to me.'"

**Memory hook:** Eleven = his eleven brothers (and father and mother in the dream). One dream, one Joseph — the beloved son whose brothers would one day bow; the number 11 locks the dream in place.

**Why this number?** 11 = his eleven brothers (Genesis 37:9). The dream of sun, moon, and eleven stars foretells the family bowing to Joseph in Egypt — one number, one vivid scene.
