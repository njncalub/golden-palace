# 68 — Person: Barak

**Number:** 68

**Person:** Barak

**Action:** Marching with Deborah to battle (Mount Tabor, against Sisera)

**Object:** Army / Mount Tabor

**Scripture connection:** Judges 4:6 — She sent and summoned Barak the son of Abinoam from Kedesh-naphtali and said to him, "Has not the LORD, the God of Israel, commanded you, 'Go, gather your men at Mount Tabor'?" Judges 4:14 — And Deborah said to Barak, "Up! For this is the day in which the LORD has given Sisera into your hand."

**Memory hook:** 68 = Chet-Vav = fence + nail; Barak (Judg 4). Barak marched with Deborah; the number 68 ties him to Mount Tabor and the army.
