# 90 — Person: Dorcas (Tabitha)

**Number:** 90

**Person:** Dorcas (Tabitha)

**Action:** Making tunics and other garments for the widows

**Object:** Tunics / sewing

**Scripture connection:** Acts 9:36 — Now there was in Joppa a disciple named Tabitha, which, translated, means Dorcas. She was full of good works and acts of charity. Acts 9:39 — So Peter rose and went with them. And when he arrived, they took him to the upper room. All the widows stood beside him weeping and showing tunics and other garments that Dorcas made while she was with them.

**Memory hook:** 90 = Tet = basket; Acts 9:36. Dorcas and the tunics — the number 90 is the disciple full of good works whom Peter raised.

**Why this number?** 90 = Tet = basket; Acts 9:36. Dorcas made tunics for the widows; the number 90 ties her to the Hebrew letter and the sewing.
