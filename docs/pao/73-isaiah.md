# 73 — Person: Isaiah

**Number:** 73

**Person:** Isaiah

**Action:** Touching the live coal to his lips (in the temple vision)

**Object:** Live coal

**Scripture connection:** Isaiah 6:6–7 — Then one of the seraphim flew to me, having in his hand a burning coal that he had taken with tongs from the altar. And he touched my mouth and said: "Behold, this has touched your lips; your guilt is taken away, and your sin atoned for."

**Memory hook:** 73 = Zayin-Gimel = weapon + grace; Isa 6. Isaiah and the coal — the prophet whose lips were cleansed. The number 73 ties him to the vision and the altar.

**Why this number?** 73 = Zayin-Gimel = weapon + grace; Isa 6. Isaiah touched the coal to his lips; the number 73 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the live coal.
