# 21 — Person: Jacob

**Number:** 21

**Person:** Jacob

**Action:** Seeing the ladder at Bethel — angels ascending and descending

**Object:** Stone pillow / ladder

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 28:12–13 — He dreamed, and behold a ladder set up on the earth, and the top of it reached to heaven; and behold the angels of God were ascending and descending on it. And behold the LORD stood above it.

**Memory hook:** 21 = Bet (house) + Aleph (God) — house of God (Bethel). Jacob at Bethel — the stone for a pillow, the ladder, the promise. One night, one place where heaven opened.

**Why this number?** 21 = Bet-Aleph — house of God (Genesis 28). Jacob at Bethel sees the ladder; the number 21 is the house of God where the covenant is renewed.
