# 37 — Person: Joseph (in Egypt)

**Number:** 37

**Person:** Joseph (in Egypt, vizier)

**Action:** Ruling; his brothers bowing before him

**Object:** Signet / chariot

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 41:42–43 — Pharaoh took his signet ring from his hand and put it on Joseph's hand … and he made him ride in his second chariot. Genesis 42:6 — Joseph's brothers came and bowed themselves before him with their faces to the ground.

**Memory hook:** 37 = gematria of unity; Joseph the one who reunites the family. Genesis 37–50 — the dreamer who rules and forgives; one number, one story of reconciliation.

**Why this number?** 37 = gematria unity; Genesis 37–50. Joseph in Egypt — the signet, the chariot, the brothers bowing; the number 37 is the man who saved the house of Israel.
