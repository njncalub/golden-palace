# 28 — Person: Nicodemus

**Number:** 28

**Person:** Nicodemus

**Action:** Coming to Jesus by night (asking about being born again)

**Object:** Night

**Scripture connection:** John 3 — Nicodemus, a ruler of the Jews, came to Jesus by night and said, "Rabbi, we know that you are a teacher come from God." Jesus said, "Unless one is born again he cannot see the kingdom of God."

**Memory hook:** John 3 — teacher of Israel, by night. The number 28 is the night visit — the Pharisee in the dark, the Light who answers. One conversation, one birth from above.

**Why this number?** John 3 — Nicodemus, teacher of Israel, comes by night. The number 28 ties the ruler and the nighttime dialogue to the new birth.
