# 44 — Person: Peter

**Number:** 44

**Person:** Peter

**Action:** Preaching at Pentecost — "Repent and be baptized"

**Object:** Keys / Spirit

**Scripture connection:** Acts 2:14 — But Peter, standing with the eleven, lifted up his voice and addressed them. Acts 2:38 — "Repent and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins, and you will receive the gift of the Holy Spirit."

**Memory hook:** 44 = Acts — Peter in the early chapters; Pentecost, the keys of the kingdom (Matthew 16:19). The number 44 is the rock who opened the door to the Gentiles.

**Why this number?** 44 = Acts; Peter in Acts (44). Peter preaches at Pentecost; the number 44 ties the apostle to the birth of the church.
