# 41 — Person: Mark (John Mark)

**Number:** 41

**Person:** Mark (John Mark)

**Action:** Writing the Gospel (the beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ)

**Object:** Scroll / Gospel

**Scripture connection:** Mark 1:1 — The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God. Acts 12:12 — He went to the house of Mary, the mother of John whose other name was Mark.

**Memory hook:** 41 = the Gospel of Mark — the second Gospel, fast-paced and vivid. One author, one scroll; the number 41 is the gospel that runs.

**Why this number?** 41 = Gospel of Mark (41). Mark (John Mark) is the one who wrote the earliest Gospel; the number 41 ties the book to the man.
