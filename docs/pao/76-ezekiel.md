# 76 — Person: Ezekiel

**Number:** 76

**Person:** Ezekiel

**Action:** Seeing the wheel within wheel (the vision of the throne and the glory)

**Object:** Wheel / throne

**Scripture connection:** Ezekiel 1:15–16 — As I looked at the living creatures, I saw a wheel on the earth beside the living creatures, one for each of the four of them. As for the appearance of the wheels and their construction: their appearance was like the gleaming of beryl. And the four had the same likeness, their appearance and construction being as it were a wheel within a wheel.

**Memory hook:** 76 = Zayin-Vav = weapon + nail; Ezek 1. Ezekiel and the wheels — the number 76 is the prophet by the Chebar and the vision of the glory.

**Why this number?** 76 = Zayin-Vav = weapon + nail; Ezek 1. Ezekiel saw the wheel within wheel; the number 76 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the throne vision.
