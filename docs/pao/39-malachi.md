# 39 — Person: Malachi

**Number:** 39

**Person:** Malachi

**Action:** Prophesying the messenger of the covenant — "Behold, I send my messenger"

**Object:** Scroll

**Scripture connection:** Malachi 3:1 — "Behold, I send my messenger, and he will prepare the way before me. And the Lord whom you seek will suddenly come to his temple."

**Memory hook:** 39 = last book of the OT; Malachi 3:1 — the bridge to the New. One prophet, one scroll; the number 39 is the final word before the silence until John the Baptist.

**Why this number?** 39 = last book OT; Malachi 3:1 (39). The number 39 ties Malachi to the messenger and the coming of the Lord.
