# 72 — Person: Elisha

**Number:** 72

**Person:** Elisha

**Action:** Picking up Elijah's mantle (after Elijah was taken up)

**Object:** Mantle

**Scripture connection:** 2 Kings 2:13 — And Elisha picked up the cloak of Elijah that had fallen from him and went back and stood on the bank of the Jordan.

**Memory hook:** 72 = Zayin-Bet = weapon/crown + house; 2 Kgs 2. Elisha took the mantle and struck the water; the number 72 is the successor and the cloak.

**Why this number?** 72 = Zayin-Bet = weapon/crown + house; 2 Kgs 2. Elisha picked up Elijah's mantle; the number 72 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the mantle.
