# 75 — Person: Daniel

**Number:** 75

**Person:** Daniel

**Action:** In the lions' den (the LORD shut the mouths of the lions)

**Object:** Lions

**Scripture connection:** Daniel 6:16 — Then the king commanded, and Daniel was brought and cast into the den of lions. Daniel 6:22 — My God sent his angel and shut the lions' mouths, and they have not harmed me.

**Memory hook:** 75 = Zayin-Hey = weapon + behold; Dan 6. Daniel in the den — the number 75 is the man who prayed and was delivered from the lions.

**Why this number?** 75 = Zayin-Hey = weapon + behold; Dan 6. Daniel was in the lions' den; the number 75 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the lions.
