# 93 — Person: Philip (evangelist)

**Number:** 93

**Person:** Philip (evangelist, one of the Seven)

**Action:** Baptizing the Ethiopian eunuch (in the chariot, reading Isaiah)

**Object:** Chariot / scroll

**Scripture connection:** Acts 8:28 — He was reading the prophet Isaiah. … Acts 8:35 — Then Philip opened his mouth, and beginning with this Scripture he told him the good news about Jesus. Acts 8:38 — And he commanded the chariot to stop, and they both went down into the water, Philip and the eunuch, and he baptized him.

**Memory hook:** 93 = Tet-Gimel = basket + journey; Acts 8. Philip and the chariot — the number 93 is the evangelist on the road who baptized the Ethiopian.

**Why this number?** 93 = Tet-Gimel = basket + journey; Acts 8. Philip baptized the Ethiopian in the chariot; the number 93 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the scroll.
