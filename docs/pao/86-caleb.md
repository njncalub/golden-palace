# 86 — Person: Caleb

**Number:** 86

**Person:** Caleb

**Action:** Spying the land; claiming Hebron (the mountain the LORD promised)

**Object:** Cluster of grapes

**Scripture connection:** Numbers 13:23 — And they came to the Valley of Eshcol and cut down from there a branch with a single cluster of grapes, and they carried it on a pole between two of them. Joshua 14:12 — So now give me this mountain of which the LORD spoke on that day, for you heard on that day how the Anakim were there. … It may be that the LORD will be with me, and I shall drive them out.

**Memory hook:** 86 = Chet-Vav = fence + nail; Num 13–14. Caleb and the cluster — the number 86 is the spy who followed the LORD wholeheartedly and received Hebron.

**Why this number?** 86 = Chet-Vav = fence + nail; Num 13–14. Caleb spied the land and claimed Hebron; the number 86 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the grapes.
