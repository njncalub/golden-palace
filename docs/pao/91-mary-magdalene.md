# 91 — Person: Mary Magdalene

**Number:** 91

**Person:** Mary Magdalene

**Action:** At the empty tomb (first to see the risen Lord in John's account)

**Object:** Tomb / spices

**Scripture connection:** John 20:1 — Now on the first day of the week Mary Magdalene came to the tomb early, while it was still dark, and saw that the stone had been taken away from the tomb. John 20:14–16 — She turned around and saw Jesus standing … Jesus said to her, "Mary." She turned and said to him in Aramaic, "Rabboni!"

**Memory hook:** 91 = Tet-Aleph = basket + first; John 20. Mary Magdalene at the tomb — the number 91 is the first witness to the resurrection in John.

**Why this number?** 91 = Tet-Aleph = basket + first; John 20. Mary Magdalene was at the empty tomb; the number 91 ties her to the Hebrew letters and the spices.
