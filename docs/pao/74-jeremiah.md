# 74 — Person: Jeremiah

**Number:** 74

**Person:** Jeremiah

**Action:** Burying the waistband by the Euphrates (the sign of ruined pride)

**Object:** Waistband / Euphrates

**Scripture connection:** Jeremiah 13:4–5 — Take the waistband that you have bought … and go to the Euphrates and hide it there in a cleft of the rock. So I went and hid it by the Euphrates, as the LORD commanded me.

**Memory hook:** 74 = Zayin-Dalet = weapon + door; Jer 13. Jeremiah and the waistband — the prophet who buried the sign. The number 74 ties him to the Euphrates and the waistband.

**Why this number?** 74 = Zayin-Dalet = weapon + door; Jer 13. Jeremiah buried the waistband; the number 74 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the object.
