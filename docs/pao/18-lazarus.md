# 18 — Person: Lazarus

**Number:** 18

**Person:** Lazarus

**Action:** Raised from the dead — coming out of the tomb at Jesus' command

**Object:** Grave clothes

**Scripture connection:** John 11:43–44 — Jesus cried out with a loud voice, "Lazarus, come out." The man who had died came out, his hands and feet bound with grave clothes, and his face wrapped with a cloth.

**Memory hook:** 18 = chai (חי) — life, in Hebrew. Lazarus is the one who was dead and lived again; the number 18 is life — one man, one resurrection before the cross.

**Why this number?** 18 = chai (life) in Hebrew; John 11. Lazarus is the man raised to life; the number 18 locks the miracle to the word for life.
