# 46 — Person: Apollos

**Number:** 46

**Person:** Apollos

**Action:** Preaching in Corinth (mighty in the Scriptures)

**Object:** Scripture

**Scripture connection:** Acts 18:24–25 — Now a Jew named Apollos, a native of Alexandria, came to Ephesus. He was an eloquent man, competent in the Scriptures … he spoke and taught accurately the things concerning Jesus. 1 Corinthians 1:12 — "I follow Apollos."

**Memory hook:** 46 = 1 Corinthians — Apollos was a teacher in Corinth (Acts 18; 1 Cor). The number 46 is the eloquent man, mighty in the Scriptures.

**Why this number?** 46 = 1 Corinthians; Apollos (Acts 18; 1 Cor). The number 46 ties Apollos to Corinth and the letter that mentions him.
