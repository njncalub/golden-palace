# 56 — Person: Titus

**Number:** 56

**Person:** Titus

**Action:** Appointing elders in every town (in Crete, as Paul charged)

**Object:** Crete / elders

**Scripture connection:** Titus 1:5 — This is why I left you in Crete, so that you might put what remained into order, and appoint elders in every town as I directed you.

**Memory hook:** 56 = Titus; Titus 1:5 — the concrete action of appointing elders in every town. The number 56 is the pastor left in Crete to set the church in order.

**Why this number?** 56 = Titus; Titus 1:5 — concrete action. Titus appointed elders in every town in Crete; the number 56 ties him to the letter that bears his name.
