# 95 — Person: Zacchaeus

**Number:** 95

**Person:** Zacchaeus

**Action:** Climbing the sycamore tree (to see Jesus)

**Object:** Sycamore tree

**Scripture connection:** Luke 19:4 — So he ran on ahead and climbed up into a sycamore tree to see him, for he was about to pass that way. Luke 19:5 — And when Jesus came to the place, he looked up and said to him, "Zacchaeus, hurry and come down, for I must stay at your house today."

**Memory hook:** 95 = Tet-Hey = basket + behold; Luke 19. Zacchaeus in the sycamore — the number 95 is the chief tax collector who climbed to see and was seen by Jesus.

**Why this number?** 95 = Tet-Hey = basket + behold; Luke 19. Zacchaeus climbed the sycamore; the number 95 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the tree.
