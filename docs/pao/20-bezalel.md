# 20 — Person: Bezalel

**Number:** 20

**Person:** Bezalel

**Action:** Building the tabernacle (crafting boards, furnishings)

**Object:** Tabernacle boards

**Scripture connection:** Exodus 26:18 — You shall make the boards for the tabernacle; Exodus 31:2–5 — Bezalel filled with the Spirit of God, with skill to work in gold, silver, bronze, stone, wood.

**Memory hook:** 20 = the twenty boards of the tabernacle (Exodus 26:18). Bezalel the craftsman — Spirit-filled, hands that build the dwelling place of God. One man, one sanctuary.

**Why this number?** 20 boards of the tabernacle (Exodus 26:18). Bezalel is the one who built the tabernacle; the number 20 ties the builder to the structure.
