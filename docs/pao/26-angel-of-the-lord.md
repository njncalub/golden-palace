# 26 — Person: The Angel of the LORD

**Number:** 26

**Person:** The Angel of the LORD

**Action:** Appearing to Moses in the flame (or to Abraham with the ram)

**Object:** Flame / ram

**Scripture connection:** Exodus 3:2 — The angel of the LORD appeared to him in a flame of fire out of the midst of a bush. Genesis 22:11 — The angel of the LORD called to Abraham from heaven. The divine presence in angelic form.

**Memory hook:** 26 = gematria of YHWH (the tetragrammaton). The Angel of the LORD is the visible manifestation of the Name — flame, ram, the one who speaks for God. One number, one Name.

**Why this number?** 26 = gematria YHWH. The Angel of the LORD is the theophany — the one who bears the Name; the number 26 ties the appearance to the divine name.
