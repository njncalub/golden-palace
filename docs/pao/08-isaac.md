# 08 — Person: Isaac

**Number:** 08

**Person:** Isaac (as infant)

**Action:** Circumcised on the eighth day

**Object:** Covenant sign

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 21:4 — "When his son Isaac was eight days old, Abraham circumcised him, as God commanded him."

**Memory hook:** Chet = fence, inner room — the covenant sets God's people apart (fence). Isaac is the child of promise, marked on the eighth day like every Israelite male after him (Lev 12:3).

**Why this number?** 8 maps to Chet (ח) — fence, inner room. Circumcision is the covenant "fence" that marks God's people. Isaac was circumcised on the eighth day (Genesis 21:4), the pattern for all Israel. Eight = covenant sign and new beginning.
