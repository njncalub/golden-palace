# 31 — Person: The virtuous woman

**Number:** 31

**Person:** The virtuous woman (capable wife of Proverbs 31)

**Action:** Working with her hands — spinning, managing the household

**Object:** Spindle / household

**Scripture connection:** Proverbs 31:13, 19 — She seeks wool and flax and works with willing hands; she puts her hands to the distaff, and her hands hold the spindle.

**Memory hook:** 31 = Proverbs 31 — the capable wife. One chapter, one portrait; the number 31 is the woman who fears the LORD.

**Why this number?** Proverbs 31 — the capable wife. The number 31 ties the portrait of the virtuous woman to the final chapter of Proverbs.
