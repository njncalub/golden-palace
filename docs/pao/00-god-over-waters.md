# 00 — Person: God (the LORD)

**Number:** 00

**Person:** God (the LORD)

**Action:** Moving over the face of the waters — the moment before "Let there be light"

**Object:** The deep (tehom)

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 1:2 — "The earth was formless and empty, and darkness was over the surface of the deep, and the Spirit of God was moving over the face of the waters."

**Memory hook:** Zero = no creation yet. You see the deep, dark waters and God moving over them — one clear Actor, one clear scene — before the first day begins.

**Why this number?** The digit 0 has no Hebrew letter — it is the void, the "nothing" before counting. Genesis 1:2 gives the peg: God (ruach Elohim, His Spirit) moving over the face of the waters before day one. One Person (God), one action (moving over), one object (the deep).
