# 27 — Person: Stephen

**Number:** 27

**Person:** Stephen

**Action:** Preaching before the council; stones falling upon him

**Object:** Stones

**Scripture connection:** Acts 7 — Stephen's speech before the Sanhedrin; Acts 7:58 — they cast him out of the city and stoned him. The first martyr — he saw the heavens opened and the Son of Man standing.

**Memory hook:** 27 = Bet-Zayin — house + weapon. Stephen the first martyr — the stones that kill him are the weapon; the house (council) that condemns him. One man, one testimony unto death.

**Why this number?** 27 = Bet-Zayin; first martyr (Acts 7). Stephen is stoned; the number 27 ties the deacon and martyr to the Hebrew letter pair.
