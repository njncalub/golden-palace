# 35 — Person: Habakkuk

**Number:** 35

**Person:** Habakkuk

**Action:** Standing on the watchtower; rejoicing though the fig tree does not blossom

**Object:** Watchtower

**Scripture connection:** Habakkuk 2:1 — I will take my stand at my watchpost; Habakkuk 2:4 — "The righteous shall live by his faith"; Habakkuk 3:17–19 — Though the fig tree should not blossom … yet I will rejoice in the LORD.

**Memory hook:** Hab 2:4; 3:17–19 — righteous by faith. The number 35 is the prophet on the watchtower who learns to rejoice in God alone.

**Why this number?** Habakkuk 2:4; 3:17–19 — the righteous live by faith; the number 35 ties the prophet to the watchtower and the song of trust.
