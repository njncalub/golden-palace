# 47 — Person: Timothy

**Number:** 47

**Person:** Timothy

**Action:** Carrying letters; shepherding the churches with Paul

**Object:** Letters

**Scripture connection:** 2 Corinthians 1:1 — Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God, and Timothy our brother. 1 Timothy 1:2 — To Timothy, my true child in the faith. Philippians 2:19–22 — I hope in the Lord Jesus to send Timothy to you soon.

**Memory hook:** 47 = 2 Corinthians — Timothy is named as co-sender. The number 47 is the son in the faith who carried Paul's letters and pastored Ephesus.

**Why this number?** 47 = 2 Corinthians; Timothy (47). Timothy carried letters and shepherded; the number 47 ties him to the second letter to Corinth.
