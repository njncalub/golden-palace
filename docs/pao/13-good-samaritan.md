# 13 — Person: The Good Samaritan

**Number:** 13

**Person:** The Good Samaritan

**Action:** Binding wounds, pouring oil and wine, showing love to the stranger

**Object:** Oil, wine, beast (animal for the journey)

**Scripture connection:** Luke 10:33–34 — A Samaritan came where the man was, had compassion, went to him, bound his wounds, poured oil and wine, set him on his own animal, brought him to an inn.

**Memory hook:** 13 = gematria of love (ahavah) and one (echad). One who loved his neighbor as himself — the outsider who did what the religious passed by. One action, one mercy.

**Why this number?** 13 ties to the gematria of love (ahavah) and one (echad). The Good Samaritan is the one who showed covenant love to the wounded man — one number, one parable of neighbor-love.
