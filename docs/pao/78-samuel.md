# 78 — Person: Samuel

**Number:** 78

**Person:** Samuel

**Action:** Anointing David king (in the midst of his brothers)

**Object:** Horn of oil

**Scripture connection:** 1 Samuel 16:13 — Then Samuel took the horn of oil and anointed him in the midst of his brothers. And the Spirit of the LORD rushed upon David from that day forward.

**Memory hook:** 78 = Zayin-Chet = weapon + fence; 1 Sam 16. Samuel and the horn of oil — the number 78 is the prophet who anointed the shepherd boy king.

**Why this number?** 78 = Zayin-Chet = weapon + fence; 1 Sam 16. Samuel anointed David with the horn of oil; the number 78 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the anointing.
