# 45 — Person: Paul

**Number:** 45

**Person:** Paul

**Action:** Writing to the Romans (the letter that explains the gospel of righteousness by faith)

**Object:** Letter / scroll

**Scripture connection:** Romans 1:1 — Paul, a servant of Christ Jesus, called to be an apostle. Romans 1:16 — "For I am not ashamed of the gospel, for it is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes."

**Memory hook:** 45 = Romans — the letter to the capital; the gospel in systematic form. One apostle, one letter; the number 45 is Paul and the righteousness of God.

**Why this number?** 45 = Romans (45). Paul wrote the letter to the Romans; the number 45 ties the epistle to the apostle and the gospel.
