# 69 — Person: Gideon

**Number:** 69

**Person:** Gideon

**Action:** Laying out the fleece (testing the LORD's word — dew on the fleece, dry ground)

**Scripture connection:** Judges 6:36–40 — Then Gideon said to God, "If you will save Israel by my hand … let me test … with the fleece." … And God did so that night; and it was dry on the fleece only, and on all the ground there was dew.
