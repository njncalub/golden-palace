# 12 — Person: James (son of Zebedee)

**Number:** 12

**Person:** James (son of Zebedee)

**Action:** Mending nets in the boat with John

**Object:** Nets / boat

**Scripture connection:** Mark 1:19 — Jesus saw James son of Zebedee and his brother John in a boat, mending the nets; Matthew 10:2 — James is one of the twelve apostles.

**Memory hook:** Twelve = the twelve apostles; James is one of the Twelve. Nets and boat — the fisherman called to become a fisher of men; one of the inner three with Peter and John.

**Why this number?** 12 = one of the twelve apostles (Mark 1:19; Matthew 10:2). James son of Zebedee is fixed at 12 — the apostolic band, the boat, the nets left behind.
