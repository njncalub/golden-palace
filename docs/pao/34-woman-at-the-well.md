# 34 — Person: The woman at the well

**Number:** 34

**Person:** The woman at the well (Samaritan woman, Sychar)

**Action:** Drawing water; talking to Jesus — "Give me this water"

**Object:** Water jar

**Scripture connection:** John 4:7 — A woman from Samaria came to draw water. Jesus said to her, "Give me a drink." John 4:28–29 — The woman left her water jar and went into town and said to the people, "Come, see a man who told me all that I ever did."

**Memory hook:** John 4 — the Samaritan woman at Jacob's well. The number 34 is the woman who met the Living Water and became a messenger to her city.

**Why this number?** John 4 — the woman at the well (Sychar). The number 34 ties the encounter to the Gospel and the gift of living water.
