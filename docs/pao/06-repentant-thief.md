# 06 — Person: The repentant thief

**Number:** 06

**Person:** The repentant thief (on the cross beside Jesus)

**Action:** Looking at Jesus and asking to be remembered in His kingdom

**Object:** Cross / nails

**Scripture connection:** Luke 23:39–43 — One criminal rebuked the other; "Jesus, remember me when you come into your kingdom"; Jesus said, "Today you will be with me in paradise."

**Memory hook:** Vav = nail, hook, connection. He was nailed beside the Savior and connected to Him by faith in his last moments. Six is the number of the nails that held them there.

**Why this number?** 6 maps to Vav (ו) — nail, hook, connection. The thief was crucified — nailed — beside Jesus and, through faith, was connected to Him forever. He is the only person in Scripture explicitly promised paradise that same day.
