# 48 — Person: Barnabas

**Number:** 48

**Person:** Barnabas

**Action:** Laying the money from the sale of land at the apostles' feet

**Object:** Money / field

**Scripture connection:** Acts 4:36–37 — Thus Joseph, who was also called by the apostles Barnabas (which means son of encouragement), a Levite, a native of Cyprus, sold a field that belonged to him and brought the money and laid it at the apostles' feet.

**Memory hook:** 48 = Galatians — Barnabas was with Paul in the early mission; he sold the field in Acts 4. The number 48 is the son of encouragement and the gift at the apostles' feet.

**Why this number?** 48 = Galatians; Barnabas sold field, gave proceeds (Acts 4:36–37). The number 48 ties Barnabas to the act of laying money at the apostles' feet.
