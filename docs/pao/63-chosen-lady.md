# 63 — Person: The chosen lady

**Number:** 63

**Person:** The chosen lady (recipient of 2 John)

**Action:** Receiving the elder's letter (and her children walking in the truth)

**Object:** Letter

**Scripture connection:** 2 John 1 — The elder to the elect lady and her children, whom I love in truth. 2 John 4 — I rejoiced greatly to find some of your children walking in the truth.

**Memory hook:** 63 = 2 John; one recipient — concrete: receiving the letter. The number 63 is the chosen lady and her children who walk in the truth.

**Why this number?** 63 = 2 John; one recipient — concrete: receiving the letter. The number 63 ties the chosen lady to the second letter of John.
