# 77 — Person: Samson

**Number:** 77

**Person:** Samson

**Action:** Striking the Philistines with the jawbone of a donkey

**Object:** Jawbone of donkey

**Scripture connection:** Judges 15:15 — And he found a fresh jawbone of a donkey, and put out his hand and took it, and struck 1,000 men with it.

**Memory hook:** 77 = Zayin-Zayin = double weapon; Judg 15. Samson and the jawbone — the number 77 is the judge and the weapon in his hand.

**Why this number?** 77 = Zayin-Zayin = double weapon; Judg 15. Samson struck with the jawbone; the number 77 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the jawbone.
