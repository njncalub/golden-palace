# 32 — Person: Jonah

**Number:** 32

**Person:** Jonah

**Action:** In the belly of the great fish (three days and three nights)

**Object:** The great fish

**Scripture connection:** Jonah 1:17 — And the LORD appointed a great fish to swallow Jonah, and Jonah was in the belly of the fish three days and three nights.

**Memory hook:** 32 = 3 + 2 — three days in the fish; the prophet who ran and was swallowed. One man, one fish, one resurrection sign (Matthew 12:40).

**Why this number?** 32 suggests the three days (Jonah 1:17); the number locks Jonah to the sign of the fish and the grave.
