# 23 — Person: Boaz

**Number:** 23

**Person:** Boaz

**Action:** Spreading his cloak over Ruth (redeeming her)

**Object:** Grain / cloak

**Scripture connection:** Ruth 3:9 — Ruth said, "Spread your wings over your servant, for you are a redeemer." Ruth 3:15 — Boaz gave her six measures of barley. Boaz is the kinsman-redeemer.

**Memory hook:** 23 = Bet (house) + Gimel (grace) — house of grace. Boaz covers Ruth with his cloak; the grain, the threshing floor, the redemption. One man, one act of covenant love.

**Why this number?** 23 = Bet-Gimel — house + grace (Ruth 3). Boaz spreads his cloak over Ruth; the number 23 is the house of grace and redemption.
