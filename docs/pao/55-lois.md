# 55 — Person: Lois

**Number:** 55

**Person:** Lois (grandmother of Timothy)

**Action:** Nurturing young Timothy in the Scriptures (faith that dwelt first in Lois)

**Object:** Child Timothy / scroll

**Scripture connection:** 2 Timothy 1:5 — I am reminded of your sincere faith, a faith that dwelt first in your grandmother Lois and your mother Eunice and now, I am sure, dwells in you as well.

**Memory hook:** 55 = 2 Timothy; Lois, grandmother (2 Tim 1:5). The number 55 is the grandmother who passed the faith to Timothy — concrete: nurturing the child with the scroll.

**Why this number?** 55 = 2 Timothy; Lois, grandmother (2 Tim 1:5). Lois nurtured Timothy in the faith; the number 55 ties her to the second letter to Timothy.
