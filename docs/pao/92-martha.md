# 92 — Person: Martha

**Number:** 92

**Person:** Martha

**Action:** Serving; "Lord, if you had been here, my brother would not have died"

**Object:** Household / meal

**Scripture connection:** Luke 10:40 — But Martha was distracted with much serving. John 11:21 — Martha said to Jesus, "Lord, if you had been here, my brother would not have died." John 11:27 — She said to him, "Yes, Lord; I believe that you are the Christ, the Son of God."

**Memory hook:** 92 = Tet-Bet = basket + house; Luke 10; John 11. Martha serving — the number 92 is the woman of the household who confessed the Christ.

**Why this number?** 92 = Tet-Bet = basket + house; Luke 10; John 11. Martha served and confessed; the number 92 ties her to the Hebrew letters and the meal.
