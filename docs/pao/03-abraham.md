# 03 — Person: Abraham

**Number:** 03

**Person:** Abraham

**Action:** Offering Isaac on the altar (or welcoming three visitors)

**Object:** The altar / knife / ram in the thicket

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 22:9–10 — Abraham bound Isaac and laid him on the altar; Genesis 18:2 — three men (the LORD and two angels) appeared to him.

**Memory hook:** Journey (Gimel = camel), grace (God provides the ram), and the test of the father. Three visitors at Mamre; three-day journey to Moriah.

**Why this number?** 3 maps to Gimel (ג) — camel, journey, grace. Abraham is the man of the journey (called to leave Ur) and of grace (God provides the lamb). Three visitors announce Isaac's birth; the binding of Isaac points to the grace of substitution.
