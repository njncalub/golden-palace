# 29 — Person: Joel (prophet)

**Number:** 29

**Person:** Joel (prophet)

**Action:** Prophesying the Spirit poured out on all flesh

**Object:** Spirit / locusts

**Scripture connection:** Joel 2:28–29 — "And it shall come to pass afterward, that I will pour out my Spirit on all flesh; your sons and your daughters shall prophesy, your old men shall dream dreams, and your young men shall see visions."

**Memory hook:** Joel 2:28–29 — the promise of the poured-out Spirit. Locusts and judgment give way to the promise; the number 29 ties the prophet to the verses that point to Pentecost (Acts 2).

**Why this number?** Joel 2:28–29 — "I will pour out my Spirit." The number 29 locks the prophet to the promise of the Spirit poured out, fulfilled at Pentecost.
