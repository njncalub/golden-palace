# 65 — Person: Jude

**Number:** 65

**Person:** Jude (brother of James, servant of Jesus Christ)

**Action:** Writing his short letter to the church — "Contend for the faith"

**Object:** Scroll / letter

**Scripture connection:** Jude 1 — Jude, a servant of Jesus Christ and brother of James, To those who are called. Jude 3 — Beloved, although I was very eager to write to you about our common salvation, I found it necessary to write appealing to you to contend for the faith that was once for all delivered to the saints.

**Memory hook:** 65 = Jude (65); vivid action = writing the letter. The number 65 is the brother of James who wrote to contend for the faith.

**Why this number?** 65 = Jude (65). Jude wrote his short letter; the number 65 ties the book to the man and the call to contend.
