# 66 — Person: The angel of Revelation

**Number:** 66

**Person:** The angel of Revelation (the one who showed John the vision)

**Action:** Showing John the vision — the scroll, the Lamb, the new Jerusalem

**Object:** Scroll / Lamb

**Scripture connection:** Revelation 22:8 — I, John, am the one who heard and saw these things. Revelation 1:1 — The revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave him to show to his servants the things that must soon take place. He made it known by sending his angel to his servant John.

**Memory hook:** 66 = 66th book; the angel in Rev 22 (66). The number 66 ties the angel who showed the vision to the last book of the Bible.

**Why this number?** 66 = 66th book; angel in Rev 22 (66). The angel showed John the vision; the number 66 ties Revelation to the angel and the scroll.
