# 61 — Person: Demetrius

**Number:** 61

**Person:** Demetrius

**Action:** Holding the elder's commendation (well spoken of by everyone)

**Object:** Letter / good testimony

**Scripture connection:** 3 John 12 — Demetrius has received a good testimony from everyone, and from the truth itself. We also add our testimony, and you know that our testimony is true.

**Memory hook:** 61 = 3 John; Demetrius is the one well spoken of (3 John 12). The number 61 avoids duplicating John (43) — one named recipient of commendation.

**Why this number?** 61 = 3 John; Demetrius well spoken of. Demetrius holds the elder's commendation; the number 61 ties him to the third letter of John.
