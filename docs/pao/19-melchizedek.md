# 19 — Person: Melchizedek

**Number:** 19

**Person:** Melchizedek

**Action:** Bringing bread and wine to Abraham (blessing him)

**Object:** Bread and wine

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 14:18 — Melchizedek king of Salem brought out bread and wine; he was priest of God Most High; he blessed Abraham. Psalm 19 — the heavens declare the glory of God.

**Memory hook:** 19 = Psalm 19 (the heavens declare) and the king of Salem (Genesis 14:18). Priest of the Most High — bread, wine, blessing. One mysterious figure, one number linking Torah and psalm.

**Why this number?** 19 = Psalm 19 / Genesis 14:18 — king of Salem, priest of God Most High. Melchizedek brings bread and wine; the number 19 ties the scene to the psalms and the patriarch.
