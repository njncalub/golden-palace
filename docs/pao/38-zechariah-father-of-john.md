# 38 — Person: Zechariah (father of John)

**Number:** 38

**Person:** Zechariah (father of John the Baptist)

**Action:** In the temple offering incense; struck mute until John's birth

**Object:** Incense

**Scripture connection:** Luke 1:8–9 — Now while he was serving as priest before God when his division was on duty, according to the custom of the priesthood, he was chosen by lot to enter the temple of the Lord and burn incense. Luke 1:20 — "You will be silent and unable to speak until the day that these things take place."

**Memory hook:** Luke 1 — Zechariah in the temple, the angel, the mute months. The number 38 ties the priest to the incense and the birth of the forerunner.

**Why this number?** Luke 1 — Zechariah in the temple (priestly division, incense). The number 38 locks the father of John to the moment the angel spoke.
