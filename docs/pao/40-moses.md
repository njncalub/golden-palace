# 40 — Person: Moses

**Number:** 40

**Person:** Moses

**Action:** Parting the Red Sea (staff stretched out)

**Object:** Staff

**Scripture connection:** Exodus 14:21 — Then Moses stretched out his hand over the sea, and the LORD drove the sea back by a strong east wind all night and made the sea dry land. Deuteronomy 34:7 — Moses was 120 years old; he had led Israel forty years in the wilderness.

**Memory hook:** 40 = forty years in the wilderness; forty days on Sinai. The staff, the sea, the deliverer — the number 40 is Moses and the exodus.

**Why this number?** 40 years wilderness; Exodus 14. Moses parts the Red Sea with the staff; the number 40 is the years and the deliverance.
