# 01 — Person: Adam

**Number:** 01

**Person:** Adam

**Action:** Naming the animals

**Object:** The animals / dust of the ground

**Scripture connection:** Genesis 2:7 — "Then the LORD God formed a man from the dust of the ground and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life"; Genesis 2:19–20 — Adam names the creatures.

**Memory hook:** First human, first naming, first relationship with the Creator. One = the beginning of humanity; Aleph = strength and primacy — the first man.

**Why this number?** 1 maps to Aleph (א) — ox, strength, first, beginning. Adam is the first man, the head of the human race. The number 01 is the narrative and letter beginning: one man from whom all descend (Romans 5:12–19).
