# 05 — Person: The boy (five loaves)

**Number:** 05

**Person:** The boy who had five barley loaves and two fish (John 6:9)

**Action:** Giving his lunch to Jesus (Andrew bringing him to Jesus)

**Object:** Five barley loaves and two fish

**Scripture connection:** John 6:9 — "Here is a boy with five small barley loaves and two small fish"; Jesus multiplies them to feed the five thousand.

**Memory hook:** Hey = behold, window — "Behold" the small offering that Jesus blesses. Five loaves; the fifth book of the Torah is Deuteronomy (law/giving).

**Why this number?** 5 maps to Hey (ה) — window, behold, breath. The number five is central to the feeding of the five thousand; the boy's five loaves are "beheld" by Jesus and multiplied. Five = the small gift that becomes abundance.
