# 54 — Person: Eunice

**Number:** 54

**Person:** Eunice (mother of Timothy)

**Action:** Teaching Timothy the Scriptures from childhood

**Object:** Scroll

**Scripture connection:** 2 Timothy 1:5 — I am reminded of your sincere faith, a faith that dwelt first in your grandmother Lois and your mother Eunice and now, I am sure, dwells in you as well.

**Memory hook:** 54 = 1 Timothy; Eunice is Timothy's mother (2 Tim 1:5). The number 54 is the mother who taught the Scriptures to the boy who would become Paul's son in the faith.

**Why this number?** 54 = 1 Timothy; Eunice (2 Tim 1:5) (54). Eunice taught Timothy the Scriptures; the number 54 ties her to the pastoral letters.
