# 30 — Person: Judas Iscariot

**Number:** 30

**Person:** Judas Iscariot

**Action:** Handing over Jesus for silver

**Object:** Thirty pieces of silver

**Scripture connection:** Matthew 26:15 — "What will you give me if I deliver him to you?" And they paid him thirty pieces of silver; Matthew 27:3 — When Judas saw that Jesus was condemned, he brought back the thirty pieces of silver.

**Memory hook:** 30 = the thirty pieces of silver — the price of the betrayal. One man, one purse; the number 30 is the wage of the traitor.

**Why this number?** 30 pieces of silver (Matthew 26:15; 27:3). Judas is fixed at 30 — the price paid for the One who was priceless.
