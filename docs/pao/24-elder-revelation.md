# 24 — Person: An elder (in Revelation)

**Number:** 24

**Person:** An elder (one of the twenty-four elders)

**Action:** Casting his golden crown before the Lamb

**Object:** Golden crown

**Scripture connection:** Revelation 4:4 — Around the throne were twenty-four thrones, and seated on the thrones were twenty-four elders, clothed in white garments, with golden crowns on their heads; Revelation 4:10 — the twenty-four elders cast their crowns before the throne.

**Memory hook:** 24 = the twenty-four elders (Revelation 4:4). One elder, one action — casting the crown before the Lamb. The number 24 is the council of heaven worshiping.

**Why this number?** 24 elders (Revelation 4:4); one elder, one action — casting his golden crown before the Lamb. Singular visual: one of the twenty-four in worship.
