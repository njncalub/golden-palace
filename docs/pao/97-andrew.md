# 97 — Person: Andrew

**Number:** 97

**Person:** Andrew

**Action:** Bringing the boy with the five loaves and two fish to Jesus

**Object:** Boy / loaves

**Scripture connection:** John 6:8–9 — One of his disciples, Andrew, Simon Peter's brother, said to him, "There is a boy here who has five barley loaves and two fish, but what are they for so many?"

**Memory hook:** 97 = Tet-Zayin = basket + weapon; John 6. Andrew and the boy — the number 97 is the apostle who brought the small offering that Jesus multiplied.

**Why this number?** 97 = Tet-Zayin = basket + weapon; John 6. Andrew brought the boy with loaves to Jesus; the number 97 ties him to the Hebrew letters and the loaves.
